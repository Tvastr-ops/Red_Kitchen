Matar Paneer

Matar Paneer is a classic North Indian curry that combines soft paneer cubes and tender green peas in a rich, spiced tomato-based gravy. It's a comforting dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Mix in turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Add green peas and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Gently fold in the paneer cubes and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
