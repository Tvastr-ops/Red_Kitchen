Idli

Idlis are soft, fluffy, and healthy steamed rice cakes that are a staple breakfast in South India. They are light, nutritious, and pair perfectly with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and urad dal separately. Soak the rice with fenugreek seeds and the dal in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the dal into a smooth batter and the rice into a slightly coarse batter. Mix them together with salt.
  3. Allow the batter to ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours in a warm place.
  4. Grease idli molds and pour the batter into them. Steam for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar for a wholesome meal.
