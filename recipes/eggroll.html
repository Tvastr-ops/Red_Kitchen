Egg Roll

Egg rolls are a delightful snack that combines the richness of eggs with the crunch of fresh vegetables, all wrapped in a soft tortilla. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a quick bite on the go!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 fresh tortilla or flatbread
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped vegetables (onions, bell peppers, carrots, etc.)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour in the eggs and spread them evenly to form a thin omelette.
  3. Once the omelette is cooked, place it on a tortilla. Add the chopped vegetables on top.
  4. Roll the tortilla tightly, ensuring the filling stays inside. Slice into halves if desired.
  5. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce or chutney.
