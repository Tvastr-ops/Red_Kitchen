Dosa

A crispy and savory South Indian crepe made from rice and lentils.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 4 to 6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Heat a pan and spread batter into a thin circle.
  5. Drizzle oil, cook until crisp, and serve hot.
