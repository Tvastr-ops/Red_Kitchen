Tacos

Tacos are a versatile and flavorful Mexican dish that can be customized with a variety of fillings. They are perfect for a quick meal or a festive gathering.

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked and seasoned meat or beans
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Salsa and sour cream for topping

Steps

  1. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  2. Fill each tortilla with a generous portion of meat or beans, followed by lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  3. Top with salsa and a dollop of sour cream for added flavor.
  4. Fold the tortillas in half and serve immediately. Enjoy with a side of guacamole or chips.
